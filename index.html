About

Thirty years of music. Finally released.

Dr. Niia Bishop spent three decades writing, composing, and living inside music — without releasing a single note. Then she saw someone release a song so bad it lit a fire. If he can do it, I can too. She hasn't stopped since.

L8 Blo0om is rooted in Caribbean and African dance culture — Kizomba, Zouk, Konpa, Reggae — and moves freely through Jazz, NeoSoul, and Reggaeton. The catalog is multilingual, multi-genre, and unmistakably hers: lyrical, sensual, cinematic, and alive.

From the debut It Begins Here to the Jazz trilogy Touché Pussycat, the Rockabilly fever dream Reality Check, Please., the healing frequencies of Center & Balance, and the Latin heat of Viernes La Verdad — the music keeps coming. A new release every two weeks, all summer long.

Dr. Niia Bishop — L8 Blo0om
